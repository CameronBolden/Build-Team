Build

Kitchens

Build Team specializes in all aspects of residential projects, home repairs, improvements and general construction. We are capable of handling the fine finish of any kitchen, bathrooms, basements, additions, renovations and restoration projects, from a minor repair to gutting a building we do it all.

 

With certified training as a kitchen designer, Build Team can provide design consulting for your new kitchen or bathroom. We pride ourselves on superb craftsmanship.

  Our kitchens speak for themselves and provide beautiful, functional, and accessible designs. We work hard to make the dreams of our customers come true. Accommodating the needs of our customers is always priority one.

Basements

Endless Pools

Every home needs a functional basement, which could serve as entertainment space, gym, play area or managed storage space.

 We can build a elegant lounge or a durable child friendly space. With a little bit of drywall, molding, paint and some recessed lighting, We will turn that unsightly basement into functional square footage!

We are factory trained Endless Pool installers and offer a wide range of products and services for all Endless Pool applications, designing a room, adding a room for housing the pool or placing it in the back yard, basement or garage......... we do it all.

 

Consulting

With our varied experience in all aspects of building, we are ready to consult new buyers or investors. This includes dealing with building department, historic society boards, development options, etc. For a minimal, fee the use of our services can save a lot of costly delays.
